Our Review

Nestled in the heart of Back Bay, Sprout & Root Kitchen has masterfully crafted a dining experience that proves vegan and gluten-free cuisine can be both innovative and deeply satisfying. From the moment you step inside, you're greeted by an atmosphere that perfectly balances modern sophistication with warm hospitality.

The restaurant's commitment to using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients shines through in every dish. Their signature mushroom risotto, made with organic arborio rice and a medley of local mushrooms, achieves a creamy consistency that many would swear contains dairy (it doesn't!).
